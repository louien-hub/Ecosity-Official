introducing, the Ecosity

Ecosity is a website/ application that generates searches into plants. Almost 30% of the earth’s surface is covered with forests and are home to over a billion trees. The implementation of this project solves the no poverty, no hunger, good health and well-being, quality education, climate action and life on land in accordance with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG). Planting trees is one of the most significant activities that we can do.

Problem

The problems that the project might encounter, first is that the subject that requires tree or seed planting activity is less since the school nowadays focuses on academic than outdoor activities. Also, the materials for planting activity, if the student wants to plant, is obviously limited. Lastly, the student’s location, they won’t have time to plant considering how far their location is.

read more

The Solution

The solution to the problem is our voluntary planting, all we ask in return is to continue to support this project not only to help us, but also to encourage everyone to join us to fight the spreading climate change. This simple planting is not only for us but also for the students but also to aim the zero hunger.
